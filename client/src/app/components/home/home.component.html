Scenes Scheduling

Minimize the costs of actors staying during the filming of scenes they don't participate in.

If your scenes aren't shot sequentially, you can use this tool to minimize how much time your actors spend during filming.

You can input the data using the graphical tool or paste your string.

Your results will be displayed on a Google Charts Timeline or you can get the result string clicking the button.

The solver has a timeout of five minutes.
